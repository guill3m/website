In my work at [Universal Units](https://universalunits.com), one of my responsibilities was the company image. With this in mind, during the last months of 2014 and the first half of 2015, I worked on a redesign of the company logo with the aim of fixing the readability and visibility problems of the previous one as well as defining and unifying the brand image.

This logo redesign was then accompanied by a complete redesign of the company website, as well as other sales and marketing material.
