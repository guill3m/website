[U²](https://universalunits.com/products) is [Universal Units’](https://universalunits.com) flagship product, where most of my time working at this company was invested. It is a web-based application for tax accounting and tax management trusted by major European corporations.

My role in the development of this application was that of the UI/UX Designer as well as Front-end Developer.
