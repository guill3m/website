To promote the use of the Valencian language, the [Plataforma de Normalització Lingüística](https://www.dival.es/normalitzacio/) (Platform for Linguistic Normalization) every year releases an adaptable signage/labeling pack in valencian, aimed to all kind of businesses and spaces. This labeling is then distributed to valencian businesses. This was my proposal for the 2009 edition.
