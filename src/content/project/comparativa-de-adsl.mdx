In my time working at AMP Servicios S.L.; a Spanish DSL and Fiber reseller for Jazztel, ONO, and Vodafone; one of the websites I worked on was ComparativaDeADSL.com, a web service aimed for consumers to be able to quickly compare prices and offers of the top broadband Internet distributors in Spain as well as providing general information about DSL and Fiber technologies.

I completely redesigned this site, both the design and the content management. All the content, the blog and the offers, was managed with a single WordPress instance with multiple custom post types and taxonomies.
