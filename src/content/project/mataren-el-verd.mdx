_Mataren el verd_ is a 272 pages fictionalized chronicle, by the valencian writter [Carme Miquel](https://carmemiquel.com), about the events that occured on the area of La Punta, an orchard space next to the city of Valencia, between the years 1987 and 2003, year of its complete destruction.

As the subject of this story could be controversial, to have complete freedom to treat this subject as the author felt it deserved, this book is a self-publication [financed with crowdfunding,](https://www.verkami.com/projects/3969-mataren-el-verd-una-novella-sobre-la-punta/) with a production run of 1,000 copies in its first edition.
