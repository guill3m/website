This Privacy Policy document describes which information is collected, and how it is used when you visit `https://www.guillemandreu.com` (the “Site”).

## Which information is collected.

When you visit the Site:

- No personal information is collected
- No information such as cookies is stored in the browser
- No information is shared with advertising companies
- No information is monetized

The following information is collected for analytics purposes, to better understand how the Site is being used in order to improve it:

- Page views
- Referral sources
- Visit duration
- Device (browser, operating system, device type, screen size, and language)
- Location (country, region, and city)

The information collected is irreversibly anonymized and can’t be traced back to an individual person.

## How is the information collected used.

The information collected (as described in the previous section) is used to help improve and optimise the Site, to find possible errors on the Site, or to mitigate and prevent malicious attacks on the Site.

The information collected is not used for anything else than what is described in this Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Services.

To make the Site possible and available, there are third-party services being used:

- [**Cloudflare**](https://www.cloudflare.com), to manage the domain’s DNS and handle network security and performance improvements.
- [**Vercel**](https://vercel.com), to host and serve the website.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy.

I may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to my practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

_Last updated on October 24, 2024._

For more information about my privacy practices, if you have any questions, or if you would like to make a complaint; please contact me via e-mail at [privacy@guillemandreu.com](mailto:privacy@guillemandreu.com).
