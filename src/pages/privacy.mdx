This privacy statement describes which information is collected, and how it is used when you visit **`www.guillemandreu.com`** (the “Site”).

<Callout title="The short version:">
  <ul>
    <li>I collect anonymized visitor information for analytics.</li>
    <li>I don’t share nor monetize such visitor information.</li>
  </ul>
</Callout>

## Which information is collected.

When you visit the Site, the following information is collected:

- Page views
- Actions performed on the Site
- Referral sources
- Visit duration
- Device (browser, operating system, device type, screen size, and language)
- Approximate location (country, region, and city)

The information collected is irreversibly anonymized, it can’t be traced back to an individual person.

Analytics information is collected in a self-hosted installation of [Umami](https://umami.is/), to which only I have access to.

When you visit the Site:

- No identifier cookies are used or stored
- No information is shared with advertising companies
- No visitor information is monetized

## How I use the information collected.

I use the information collected to help me improve and optimise the Site, to find possible errors on the Site, or to mitigate and prevent malicious attacks on the Site.

And honestly, out of curiosity, to know how many people are visiting the Site and what are they doing on the Site.

The information collected is not used for anything else than what is described in this document.

## Third-Party Services.

To make the Site possible and available, there are third-party services being used:

- [**Cloudflare**](https://www.cloudflare.com): to host and serve the Site, manage the domain’s DNS, and handle site security.
- [**Netlify**](https://www.netlify.com/): to host Umami (the analytics tool).
- [**Supabase**](https://supabase.com/): to host the analytics database.

## Contact.

For more information about my privacy practices, if you have any questions, or if you would like to make a complaint; please contact me via e-mail at [privacy@guillemandreu.com](mailto:privacy@guillemandreu.com).

## Changes to this privacy statement.

I may update this privacy statement from time to time in order to reflect changes to my practices; to improve the clarity of the content; or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
